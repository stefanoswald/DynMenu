What does Traffic Management mean to your business?

Your user’s experience on your site depends directly on how quickly and reliably your DNS directs them to the most available and highest performing parts of your web infrastructure. The first interaction users have with your website or application starts with a DNS query, and every experience after that depends on your website’s DNS performance. Ensure site availability and fast response with Dyn’s cloud-based traffic management solutions.

Understanding Traffic Management

Benefits & Features

  • ★ Highest availability: Dyn’s anycast network seamlessly transfers each user’s queries to an active web server, content delivery network or other endpoint. Active Failover assures that your user is successfully routed to content.
  • ★ Fastest response: With a global network spanning 5 continents, Dyn shortens the distance between your user and their desired content to provide the best possible site performance.
  • ★ Lowest TCO: As a cloud-based service, the total cost of ownership (TCO) with Dyn traffic management solutions is significantly lower than buying hardware, hiring DNS experts, and operating your own data centers.

Three important questions for businesses to ask:

Why is DNS important to my business?

For companies that leverage the Internet, business continuity depends on 100% site availability and low latency. Everything on the Internet relies on DNS: web, email, video, audio, and more. Unreliable and poor performing DNS directly affects your Internet presence's bottom line and your ability to conduct business.

Why should I outsource managed DNS?

Managing mission critical DNS requires investment in a countrywide/global network of data centers, complex DNS software, and deep expertise in areas such as security, DDoS, CDN integration, and failover.

Why should I choose Dyn over other DNS providers?

Only Dyn delivers a world class cloud-based managed DNS service, unparalleled performance, and advanced DNS features such as geolocation based load balancing. Dyn has over 15 years of experience running a global DNS service and is the number one provider of managed DNS services among Alexa 1000 websites - a database of the most visited sites in the world.
