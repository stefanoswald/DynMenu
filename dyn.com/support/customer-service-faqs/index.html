Why can’t I connect to my hostname?

There are a few common reasons why your Dyn Standard DNS hostnames may not be working properly. Here are the most common reasons:

  1. You don’t have an update client installed. You’ll need to install an update client on a computer that remains on and stays in the camera location. Here’s a link to our Update Client page: http://dyn.com/support/clients/Windows users can click here: http://dyn.com/support/clients/windows/installguide/Mac users can click here: http://dyn.com/support/clients/mac/installguide/
  2. If you don’t want to install the client on your computer then you can set it up in your router. Please call the router manufacturer for assistance if you still need help setting up the DDNS in your router. If it works, congratulations! If you’re still running into difficulties, please see the next step.

How do I cancel/disable auto renew?

You can disable auto renew by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your Dyn account here: https://account.dyn.com/entrance/
  2. Click “Auto Renew Settings” under the Billing section.
  3. Click the payment type to the right of the service you would like to shut off auto renewal for.
  4. Change the service to manual renewal and save settings.

Why can’t I connect to my Camera/DVR?

Most issues with a camera/DVR can be resolved by simply power cycling the camera/DVR system and your router. Once the power cycle is complete and if you are still having trouble connecting to your camera or DVR, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account on account.dyn.com.
  2. Verify your host name and IP address in the portal.
  3. Verify your IP address by either visiting http://www.whatsmyip.org/ or http://www.ipchicken.com.
  4. If there is a IP Mismatch:
    • If you are using an update client, verify it is running on your device.
    • If you don’t use an update client you will have to manually update the IP address in the DYN portal. (For more information on our update client please visit http://dyn.com/support/clients/)
  5. If these steps do not resolve the issue, please try our advanced steps listed below.

Check to be sure that a firewall is not blocking access. Also, port forwarding may need to be configured for this to work. Please contact the router manufacturer for assistance on port forwarding or visit portforward.com. Once you have all the steps accomplished, you will type “host:port” in your browser. An example is http://support.dyndns.biz:81.

If your cable or DSL modem was constructed in the last couple years, you may need to “bridge” the device. Modern modems have built-in firewalls that protect your network, just like routers; however, if you already have a router, the modem’s firewall is redundant. By setting the modem to bridge mode, all incoming traffic is passed downstream to your router, allowing the router to handle your network’s security.

For more information on bridging your modem (and whether or not you even need to do so), consult your modem’s documentation, contact your ISP, or simply search the web for your modem’s model number and the word “bridge” (e.g. “ModemCo W9004 bridge”).

You may find some useful information in our Dyn community forum, which you can visit at: http://www.dyncommunity.com/index.html

How do I transfer domains? How do I get the auth code for my domain? How do I disable secret registration?

To transfer your domain to a different registrar, the domain will need to be unlocked, and you will need the domain’s Authorization (EPP) code. These can both be accessed in the same place in your account.

  1. Log in to your Dyn account at https://account.dyn.com/entrance/
  2. In the left hand column labeled ‘My Services’ click ‘My Zones/Domains.’
  3. Click ‘Domain Registration’ next to the domain you want to transfer.
  4. You should now see the Authorization (EPP) code listed on the page.
  5. If the ‘Status’ is locked click the ‘Change’ button to unlock the domain.

NOTE: If you have ‘Secret Registration’ active for the domain, you will also need to disable that to transfer the domain. You can do so by clicking the link on the same screen that your Authorization (EPP) code is listed on.

Once you’ve completed those steps you shouldn’t need anything else from Dyn, you should be able to initiate the transfer with the new registrar.

I forgot my password/how do I reset my password?

If you have forgot or need to reset your password, you can reset it by visiting this link and entering your email or username: https://account.dyn.com/resetpass/

What is my account number?

To obtain your account number please follow the following steps:

  1. Log in to my account here: https://account.dyn.com/entrance/.
  2. Click on account setting in the left panel, once on account setting it should display your account information including your account number.

Why has my hostname been deleted?

The hostname was most likely associated with a free Dyn account. However, all free accounts were discontinued on Wednesday, May 7th. For more information on the discontinuation of free DynDNS accounts, please refer to our Free User Discontinuation FAQs.

Can I move my hostname from one account to another?

Yes! You can move services from one account to another.

  1. Log in to your account here: https://account.dyn.com/entrance/.
  2. Click on account setting.
  3. Click on ‘Move Services’ and follow the wizard to complete the service move.

How do I renew my Linksys registration?

To renew your Linksys camera service:

    1. Go to http://linksys.tzo.com.
    2. Select the radio button for ‘Linksys or Cisco Camera’. Then click the ‘Next’ button.
    3. Select the radio button ‘I have a WVC80N Wireless-N Internet Home Monitoring Camera’. Then click the ‘Next’ button.
    4. Select the radio button ‘I’m ready to place an order for Dyn Service’. Then click the ‘Next’ button.
    5. Select the radio button ‘Standard Service’. Then click the ‘Next’ button.
    6. In the first blank field fill in only the first part of your domain. For example, if your domain was MyCam.MyLinksysCam.com, you would only type ‘MyCam’ into the field (do not put www).

From the drop down list to the right of the field you just filled in select your domain extension. For example, if your domain was MyCam.MyLinksyscam.com you would select ‘MyLinksysCam.com’ from the drop down list (there is also a MyLinksysCAMERA.com domain extension, make you choose the correct one).

  • In the field next to ‘Your email address,’ fill in the email address associated with your account. Then click the ‘Next’ button.
  • Fill in your in your payment information and complete your order.

Can I get an SSL certificate for my hostname?

At this time, it is not possible to use an SSL certificate with a hostname made under a Dyn domain name (e.g. example.dyndns.org). You can, however, purchase an SSL certificate from an SSL issuer and use it with a top-level domain that you own (e.g. example.com). The SSL issuer handles the process of installing the certificate.

Can you help me set up port forwarding?

Unfortunately, due to the complexity and always changing firmware of third party routers we cannot support port forwarding. Please contact the router manufacturer for assistance or visit portforward.com.

Is there an update client for my cell phone or mobile device?

At this time we do not offer an update client for Cell phones/mobile devices.
