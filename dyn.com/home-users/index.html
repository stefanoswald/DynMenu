Home Users

You’re away from home and you need to quickly access a device on your home network. Dyn’s Remote Access product makes it fast and easy to do what you need to do and get back to your day, whether that be accessing files or checking your house for suspicious activity while you’re on vacation.

Why Should I Consider Dyn?

Key Differentiators

  • ★ Years of Experience: Since our humble beginnings in 2001, we have been working on building and improving upon the Remote Access product. We started as home users ourselves so we understand what features are important and how usability and availability are essential.
  • ★ In-House Support: Dyn aims to deliver a world-class customer experience to all of our customers. Our support teams work in Dyn offices across the globe to deliver the best possible support at all hours of the day.
  • ★ Biggest Footprint: Dyn has the largest presence out of all remote access providers on home routers. Our partnerships with all of the major router producers just makes at-home integration that much easier for you. Just plug in your dynamic host name and your router already knows what to do.

Who Is Dyn?

Dyn’s Remote Access product was the catalyst to what Dyn is today. It’s what we started with and was our main one focus for so many years. Since our beginnings, we’ve been working on Remote Access and finding ways to make it better and more functional for our users.

Dyn solutions are at the core of Internet performance. Through traffic management, message management, and domain services, Dyn is connecting people through the Internet and ensuring information gets where it needs to go, faster and more reliably than ever before.

Dyn is the number one provider of managed DNS services among Alexa 1000 websites, which is a database of the most visited websites in the world, and the top pure-play DNS provider to Fortune 1000 companies, according to Datanyze. They also rank Dyn among the top five email delivery providers in the Alexa 1000.

How Can Dyn Help Me?

Always Stay Connected

Having the ability to remotely access your home network can be useless if you’ve experienced frequent downtime. Dyn’s Remote Access is on the most reliable network with zero instances of blackouts, always keeping you connected.

Making It Simple

Remembering IP addresses is hard. What’s worse is that your home IP address is most likely constantly changing. With Dyn Remote Access, you can create an easy-to-remember host name to access your home computer or camera with. How about yourname.likescandy.com? Or yourname.is-a-geek.org?

Create An Internet Home Of Your Own

Don’t want to use one of our domains? You can register your own domain name with Dyn and use it with Dyn Standard DNS. Dyn is a fully accredited domain registrar and offers identity protection, like Secret Registration, to keep you safe on the web. We also provide a setup wizard.
