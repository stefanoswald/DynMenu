About Domain Services With Dyn

Dyn is a fully accredited registrar with ICANN, making it easy to create new domains or transfer existing ones.

Once established, your valuable time should be spent focusing on how to grow and optimize your domain, not on how you manage the complexities of owning it. Let Dyn take care of those details so you can focus on what’s important.

Benefits & Features

  • ★ Fully Accredited Registrar: As a fully accredited domain registrar, Dyn invests countless hours in understanding the rules and regulations placed on domains and commits itself to protecting your rights and capabilities as a domain owner.
  • ★ Identity Protection: Like many other aspects of the internet, security and personal information security is paramount. With options like Secret Registration, Dyn protects your personal information, giving you peace of mind that your sites identity is safe.
  • ★ Seamless Integration with Dyn’s DNS options: Once your domain is acquired, the next step to managing it is to ensure that visitors can find it and access it quickly. With a world renowned network, Dyn’s Traffic Management solutions seamlessly integrate with your domain in order to satisfy your need for business continuity.

Three important questions when it comes to domain services:

What more is there to a domain other than just purchasing it?

A lot. Deciding on and purchasing a domain is just the beginning of an infrastructure strategy. Ensuring business continuity, protecting your personal information and actively managing how traffic accesses your domain are the next core ingredients.

Why should I purchase my domain through Dyn?

As an Internet Performance company and with over 10 years industry experience, Dyn goes well beyond being just another domain registrar. We take pride is seeing you recognize your goals and have a team who is invested in your success.

I’ve heard I should look into DNSSEC Support. What is it and can you help?

We can. DNSSEC, or DNS Security Extensions, is a proposed solution to the issue of trust, adding new records to DNS alongside existing records. These new records are used to digitally “sign” a domain, using a method known as public key cryptography. TLDs with DNSSEC Support: .com, .net, .org, .biz & .se.
