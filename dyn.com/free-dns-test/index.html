Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this test for?

For anyone who owns a website and especially those sites that generate revenue. DNS performance has an impact on whether your visitors stay, spend money and return to your website. It can also be a cause of your website going down. Based on your DNS performance results, we can offer a solution that's right for your business, which ultimately leads to more reliable uptime and happier visitors.

What will it tell me?

Dyn's Free DNS Test Tool measures the average DNS latency of your domain and detects important DNS features like whether your DNS is globally distributed. Your test results will give you the information to determine and understand your current DNS performance, and the solutions on how to improve it.

Will Dyn share my email address or DNS test results with any third-party companies?

No! We believe in the golden rule when it comes to privacy – we wouldn’t do anything to you that we would get annoyed about if it were done to any of us. Need more reassurance? Check out our privacy policy.
