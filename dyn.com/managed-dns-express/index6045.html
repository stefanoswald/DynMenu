Scales with you as you grow
While Dyn’s traffic management solutions scale to the needs of some of the most active Internet websites (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.), customers with lower volume sites are able to have world class managed DNS at an affordable cost. Managed DNS Express accounts are designed to scale with you as you grow. What does this mean? As your web traffic continues to grow, your service bundle will scale with along with it. This allows your business to scale without you having to worry about having to manually upgrade from bundle to bundle or incurring costly overage fees. You also have the option to upgrade Managed DNS with advanced features with a single phone call.
Be prepared for the worst, even at your best
Potential natural disasters affecting web sites range from accidentally cut fiber optic lines to hurricanes. Outsourcing DNS is part of a sound disaster prevention strategy.
Keeps the bad guys at bay
Unfortunately, hacking and distributed denial of service (DDoS) attacks are serious threats to your organization’s online presence. Leverage Dyn expertise in DNS security to maintain business continuity.
Developer tools and API integration
Dyn Managed DNS Express’ SOAP and REST APIs enable the full integration and automation of tasks needed with your account, zones, and DNS records.
World-class customer support
Dyn’s DNS experts are available to help via phone, email or online. Monday through Friday, 8AM – 8PM Eastern
